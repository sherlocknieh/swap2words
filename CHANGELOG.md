# Change Log

## [1.1.1] - 2026-04-26

- add (Plus) mode, where additional separators are supported.

- Support more separators, including `,`, `;`, `|`, `"`, `<`, `>`, `=`, `<=`, `>=`, `!=`.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-04-03

- Initial release.
